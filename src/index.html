About Us

Your Reliable Edmonton Plumbing & Gasfitting Experts

Squad V Plumbing Inc. has proudly served Edmonton for over 7 years, providing dependable plumbing and gasfitting for homes, renovations, commercial facilities, property managers, and builders. Led by Eugene Vista, a Journeyman Plumber and Certified Gasfitter, our team ensures every project is completed safely, efficiently, and up to code. We handle everything from new-build rough-ins to basement improvements and hot water systems. Known for honesty, availability, and flexibility, we’ve worked with Wilvicson Global, Ardeco Design, Sehra Homes, and Weidner Apartments.

Eugene is passionate about mentoring young plumbers and staying ahead of industry innovations. His commitment to quality and customer satisfaction drives the company’s reputation for excellence. Eugene Vista Owner I Journeyman Plumber and Certified Gasfitter
